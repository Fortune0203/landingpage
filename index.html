How to create account

Verify Bvn

Launch the downloaded application, tap the "Create account" button, input your BVN and validate the BVN information provided.

Verify Details With OTP

Tap the "Continue" button to create a password for your account login and enter the four-digit OTP sent to your registered phone number.

Set A Transaction PIN

You will be required to create a four-digit transaction PIN for your account. Once that is completed, proceed to the YurWallet home page.
